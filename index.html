MPARS continually invests in all areas necessary to ensure that high quality of services are provided to customers. To this end, we have designed and constructed a series of strategically located logistics facilities as well as built a strong road transportation fleet across Asia. Mpars has facilities across New Delhi, Gurgaon, Mumbai, Pune, Chennai, Bangalore, Kolkata, Kerala, Gujarat, and Lucknow. With over a total ground coverage of 1.5 million sq.ft., Mpars handles Primary & Secondary Operations, RMA and Spare Parts Logistics. We are ISO 9001-2015 Certified and perform with strong operational excellence.
